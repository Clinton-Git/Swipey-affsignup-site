FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get revenue from the affiliate program?
You earn revenue by referring new users to Swipey AI. Each time a user you refer subscribes or makes a purchase, you receive a percentage of the revenue through our Lifetime RevShare model. Your earnings will grow as you bring in more users who remain active on the platform.
What is the difference between RevShare and CPA?
RevShare (Revenue Share) means you earn a percentage of the subscription revenue from users you refer for as long as they stay subscribed. In contrast, CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) is a one-time payment per successful referral. At Swipey AI, we focus on a RevShare model to maximize your long-term earnings
How can I get a higher payout?
To receive a higher payout, you can increase your referral volume or target higher-quality traffic. Consistent performance may also lead to higher RevShare percentages, bonuses, or exclusive incentives based on your results
What is the payout frequency?
We provide payouts on a monthly basis, ensuring you get paid regularly for your referrals. All earnings are calculated and settled at the end of each month, with multiple payout options available to suit your needs.
Why choose Swipey AI's Affiliate Program?
Swipey AI offers a unique product in a growing market, providing high retention rates and recurring revenue. Our affiliate program is transparent, with advanced tracking, a generous RevShare model, and dedicated support to help you succeed.
